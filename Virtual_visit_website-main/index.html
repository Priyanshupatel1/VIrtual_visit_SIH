    Top Trending Indian Pilgrimage Destinations

    Faith Centre

    The Faith Centre is built to deliver a spiritually evocative space.

    Vaishno Devi

    Vaishno Devi is Hindu goddess who is considered as Supreme Mother Goddess

    The Buddha Park of Ravangla

    The Buddha Park of Ravangla, also known as Tathagata Tsal

    Tridev Temple

    Varanasi is the oldest living city of the world, it being a chief centre of civilization.

    Gaya

    It is a city of historical significance and is one of the major tourist attractions in India.

    Shri Sai Baba

    The holy town of Shirdi in Maharashtra is synonymous with Sai Baba – the saint who was revered by followers of all religions.

    Bodh Gaya

    Bodh Gaya is the home of the Mahabodhi Temple, containing the Bodhi Tree, under which the Buddha attained nirvana.

    Haridwar

    Haridwar is the town of Gods. People wash away their sins in the sacred river of Ganges, albeit crowded city of Haridwar in Uttarakhand.

    Somnath Jyotirlinga is the first of the 12 Jyotirlinga of Lord Shiva included in the religious places of India.
